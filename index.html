About Us

Welcome to Prickles& Co, your local sanctuary for blossoms and bouquets. Nestled in the heart of the community since 1998, our flower shop has been the silent witness to countless stories celebrated milestones, and tender moments. Our founders, Lily and Rose, started this shop with a shared passion for botanical beauty and a dream to sprinkle a little joy into every life with the timeless language of flowers. We take pride in our hand-picked, sustainably-sourced flowers that arrive fresh from local farms every morning.
